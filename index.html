Shopr

An app for converting between your time and money, to find the true value of your purchases.

  • 1

    Income

    Enter the net income on your payslip, how often you get a payslip, and how many hours a week you work.

  • 2

    Item Price

    Choose the item type, and enter its price.

  • 3

    Results

    The app shows how much time it costs to buy the item, and how that compares to how much you will use it.
